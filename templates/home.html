LuxWarden protects your data from SQL injection attacks by filtering malicious inputs and securing database queries. It ensures your website stays safe from unauthorized access and data breaches.

Prevents attackers from injecting malicious scripts into your pages. Sanitizes and escapes output plus enforces Content Security Policy to stop XSS exploits.

Blocks suspicious IPs and limits excessive requests to prevent abuse. Ensures smooth performance and enhanced security against brute-force and DDoS attacks.
